About NJ

New Jersey is situated on the East Coast of the United States, bordered by New York to the north and northeast, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, Delaware to the southwest, and Pennsylvania to the west. The capital of New Jersey is Trenton. New Jersey is one of the smallest states which covers 8,721 square miles (22,608 kmÂ²) however, it is one of the most densely populated states in the U.S (9,261,699 Source: US Census 2022). New Jersey has a robust and diverse economy. It is known for its pharmaceutical industry, telecommunications, financial services, and a strong presence in the technology sector. The state is also home to the Port of New York and New Jersey, one of the busiest ports in the country. (Source:https://www.britannica.com/)

Liberty State Park

This Park covers an area of over 1,200 acres and offers a variety of recreational facilities, natural areas, and stunning views of the Manhattan skyline, the Statue of Liberty, and Ellis Island. It is one of the must visit places in New Jersey.
